\noindent \small Third quarter U.S. economic growth is strong and led primarily by increases in household consumption and a rebound in investment. The unemployment rate remains low and near to its long-run full-capacity level. Further economic growth may generate upward wage pressure. Inflation is likewise near to its two percent target in both the core CPI and the full index. Consumer confidence has surged in most measures, propelling stock market indices higher. The yield curve has flattened over the past month.
